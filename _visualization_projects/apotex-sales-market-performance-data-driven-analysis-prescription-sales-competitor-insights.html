" collection: visulization_projects --- Business Case:
Apotex, a leading pharmaceutical company in North America, aims to gain insights into its prescription sales and historical performance to optimize inventory planning. The company leverages Tableau to present detailed reports on sales trends, performance metrics, and competitive analysis, alongside recommendations for boosting sales.

In the analysis, the following key questions were addressed:
  • Market Share Analysis: What is Apotex’s market share compared to its competitors for each drug? (Market share is represented as a percentage of total sales, with sales measured by Rx Dollars.)
  • Geographic Distribution: How are sales distributed geographically across the total market?
  • Segment & Dosage Form Analysis: What are the sales and profits by segment and dosage form?
  • Sales Performance: What is the year-on-year growth percentage of Apotex’s sales?
  • Correlation Analysis: Is there a correlation between sales and distribution costs?

    • These insights will assist Apotex in identifying opportunities to increase sales and optimize distribution strategies.

    Dataset:
    The dataset has the following data:
  • Rx - contains orders of different drugs
  • Drug Distribution - contains distribution information to various pharmacy stores
  • Returns – contains details of the orders returned due to multiple reasons (stored closed, incorrect address)
  • Sales Managers – contains details about sales managers by region

    • See the dashboard in tableau public!



    Abbreviations used in the Dataset
  • OTC – Over the counter
  • DTP – Delivered at Terminal, DTP requires that the seller delivers goods to a terminal (port, airport, etc.) in the country of the buyer, and the seller clears customs in the buyer’s country.
  • Rx - Prescription
